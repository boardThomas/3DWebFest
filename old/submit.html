submit

Have or considering developing an amazing, engaging or shocking 3D web site? Whether your site is about gaming, commercial, non-profit or just for fun, if your web site delivers a surprising, stimulating or entertaining live real time 3D experience, we encourage you to apply to be a performer in the 3D Web Fest. If your submission is selected, you’ll have 1 to 3 minutes to do a live demonstration of your web site in front of celebrity guest judges and a live audience. You can bring your own musical accompaniment – or partner with a talented musician or DJ at one of our 3D Web Fest Mixers that will run every few weeks up to the time of the event. Though not limited to, 3D web sites that work just as well on mobile devices as they do on PCs will be “favored” (consider the possibilities of real time “audience participation”).
