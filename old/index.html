The Shift is On

We’ve all seen the web transition from a mostly text to an increasingly rich 2D image rich environment over the last few years – but now the shift to 3D is here. With the help of WebGL, an enabling technology that has only just recently become supported by virtually all web browsers and mobile devices, the move from a flat 2D Web to a 3D Web is on. Enabled by web standards and open source technologies like WebGL, X3DOM, GLAM, and three.js, and commercial technologies from those like Unity and Autodesk, creating stunning engaging 3D web sites has gone from “for rocket scientists only” to “mere mortals”.


The 3D Web Performance Festival

This experience will showcase websites that are the best mixture of music, art, and technology by those inspired by what’s possible from the best of the best of the 3D Web. The 3D Web Performance Festival will bring together the best of the 3D Web - presented as Live Performance Art – amazing, delightful, surprising and at times disturbing. See creations by artists and developers of all kinds showing off their best 3D web sites live with musical accompaniment. Then participate in a panel session with the performers on the future of the 3D Web including audience Q&A.


Event Details

The 3D Web Festival will be held on Wednesday May 13th from 700pm-1000pm at the Folsom Street Foundry, with ticket proceeds going to the oldest working theater in San Francisco – the Roxie. 3D Web Festival starts with a “Mixer” from 7-8 – with curtains up at 8. Tickets will be made available soon with a limited number available at the door.


Call for Festival Web3D Art

Have or considering developing an amazing, engaging or shocking 3D web site? Whether your site is about gaming, commercial, non-profit or just for fun, if your web site delivers a surprising, stimulating or entertaining live real time 3D experience, we encourage you to apply to be a performer in the 3D Web Performance Festival. If your submission is selected, you’ll have 1 to 3 minutes to do a live demonstration of your web site in front of celebrity guest judges and a live audience. You can bring your own musical accompaniment – or partner with a talented musician or DJ at one of our 3D Web Fest Mixers that will run every few weeks up to the time of the event. Though not limited to, 3D web sites that work just as well on mobile devices as they do on PCs will be “favored” (consider the possibilities of real time “audience participation”).
